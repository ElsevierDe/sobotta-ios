About this app

The new app for the renowned and established Sobotta Atlas of Human Anatomy by Elsevier Urban & Fischer: exploring anatomy with ease!

The Sobotta app is based on the 15th edition of the atlas and contains all figures from the 12 chapters of the 3 Volumes “General Anatomy and Musculoskeletal System”, “Internal Organs” and “Head, Neck and Neuroanatomy”.

 

Be it the specific search for anatomic structures or the choice via body regions – you always find what you look for!

An excellent image quality with a high zoom factor facilitates recognizing and learning anatomical structures. The nomenclatures can be viewed or hidden with one click on the pop-up menu “levels” or shown as pin – learning anatomy made easy!

Check your anatomical knowledge: in the figure you can either match the shown anatomical structure with the corresponding pin or select from a list of possible anatomical structures the right structure to assign to the shown pin – the interactive training mode guarantees a rapid learning success and excellent exam results!

Create your own Sobotta atlas: specific images, sections/subchapters or even a complete chapter can be summarized thematically in individual bookmarks to focus on specific topics for special exam requirements.

The notes function provides an uncomplicated tool for creating personal text notes on captions or nomenclatures with a fingertip. These can easily be retrieved via the corresponding figure or by clicking on the “my notes” button on the front page under “More...”.

 

Additionally, you can choose from three different languages: German/Latin, English/Latin, and English/English.
